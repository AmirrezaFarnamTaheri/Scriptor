# Commercial licensing

Scriptor is free for **non-commercial** use under the [GNU Affero General Public License v3.0](LICENSE).

## When you need a commercial license

Contact the maintainer for a separate commercial license if you want to:

- Use Scriptor in a proprietary product or closed-source fork without complying with AGPL source-sharing obligations
- Offer Scriptor (or a derivative) as a paid hosted service without providing corresponding source to users
- Embed Scriptor in commercial SaaS where AGPL network-copyleft is not acceptable for your business model
- Receive indemnification, priority support, or custom integration terms

**Non-commercial** personal, academic, and community use that complies with AGPL-3.0 does **not** require a commercial license.

## What to include in your inquiry

| Item | Example |
|------|---------|
| Organization | Company or institution name |
| Use case | Internal tool, embedded editor, hosted service |
| Deployment model | Desktop, self-hosted, SaaS |
| Expected scale | Number of users or seats |
| Timeline | Evaluation, pilot, or production |
